What We Do

DSC events take many forms - workshops, speaker sessions, etc. They are held roughly once a month. RSVPs and attendance are tracked through this site!

Study Jams

A longer event, a collective learning program designed to give students practical experience in Google Cloud Tools. These are created and provided by Google using QwikLabs.

Hands-on Workshop

A hands-on activity where students have come together to review technical content and write code, e.g. Code Labs.

Hackathon

An event where you break into teams and work on building a solution.

Speaker Sessions

An event where a Core Team member or guest speaker cover technical content.

How to Join
